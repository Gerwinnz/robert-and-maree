Details & Accomodation

Below are some of the places we recommend

As our wedding is being held over the Easter break, we recommend you book accommodation as early as possible. We will be arriving at Lake Tekapo on Thursday, April 2, and departing on Monday, April 6. We will be staying at Peppers Bluewater Resort, which is where the reception is to be held.

If you are there on the Friday night, April 3, you may like to join us for dinner at Reflections Restaurant (at your own expense) with the booking being an early sitting beginning around 5:30/6:00pm. You are also more than welcome to join us at Peppers for a buffet breakfast on the Sunday, April 5, at 9:30am (at own cost if you're not staying at Peppers).

Anyone interested in a round of golf of the Friday afternoon or a session at the hot pools is more than welcome to join the wedding party and family.

Lake Tekapo Holiday Homes

Discover Tekapo Accommodation

  • Email: accommodation@discovertekapo.co.nz (Prue Blake)
  • Website: www.discovertekapo.co.nz

Discover Tekapo have a number of houses both on the Peppers side of town and on the other side of the bridge. Their website is the best place to start.

They range in price from $170 for two adults (plus $30 for additional adults and $20 for children), to $400 for four adults (plus $40 for extras). Some can sleep 12 and are great for groups. As it is Easter you will need to book a minimum of 3 nights.

Prue Blake is happy to be contacted via email directly to find the best options. She has also advised that some gorgeous houses are Hillcrest Lodge A and B (22 Sibbald Lane).

Other options

The Holiday Park is also another option for guests (www.laketekapo-accommodation.co.nz),and the Chalet Boutique Motel is highly rated in customer reviews (www.thechalet.co.nz).

*It is strongly recommended that you avoid booking the Godley Hotel - it is God awful.
